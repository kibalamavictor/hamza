Where It All Began

Healinghearts charity organisation was founded in 2024 in Kireka, Banda, inspired by real-life experiences growing up in challenging environments.

Witnessing the effects of poverty, lack of emotional support, and limited opportunities on young people sparked a desire to create change. A tragic personal loss further strengthened this mission, highlighting how deeply circumstances can shape a person's life.

What started as a response to one community has now grown into a broader effort—reaching vulnerable children across different parts of Uganda.

Today, Healinghearts charity organisation is committed to identifying and supporting children in need wherever they are, giving them hope, care, and a chance at a better future.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To identify and support orphans and vulnerable children across Uganda through education, emotional care, and life-changing opportunities.

Vision

A Uganda where every child has access to support, dignity, and a future full of possibilities.
