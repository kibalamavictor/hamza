Frequently Asked Questions

What is Healinghearts charity organisation?
Healinghearts charity organisation is a non-profit organization founded in 2024, dedicated to supporting orphans and vulnerable children across Uganda through education, healthcare, and comprehensive care programs. We are based in Kireka, Banda and operate throughout Uganda.
Where are you located?
Our headquarters are located in Kireka, Banda, Kampala. However, we operate across various communities in Uganda, reaching children in different regions who need our support.

How can I donate?
You can donate through:
• Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money)
• Bank transfer to our account
• Online donation form on our website
• In-person at our office
All donation methods are secure and convenient.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes, donations to Healinghearts charity organisation are tax-deductible as we are a registered non-profit organization. You will receive a receipt for your donation that can be used for tax purposes.
How is my donation used?
Your donations are used to:
• Provide education support (school fees, supplies)
• Healthcare services for children
• Nutrition programs and meals
• Administrative costs (minimal percentage)
We maintain full transparency and provide regular impact reports.
What is child sponsorship?
Child sponsorship is a monthly commitment of UGX 50,000 that provides comprehensive support for a specific child, including education, healthcare, nutrition, and personal care. You'll receive updates about your sponsored child's progress.
How long does sponsorship last?
Sponsorship is typically a long-term commitment, ideally until the child completes their education. However, you can choose the duration that works best for you. We recommend at least one year to make a meaningful impact.
Can I communicate with my sponsored child?
Yes! Sponsors receive regular updates about their child's progress, including photos and letters. You can also send letters and small gifts to your sponsored child through our office.
How can I volunteer?
You can volunteer by:
• Teaching or tutoring children
• Organizing activities and events
• Administrative support
• Fundraising assistance
• Professional skills (medical, legal, etc.)
Contact us to discuss your skills and availability.
Do I need specific qualifications to volunteer?
While specific qualifications can be helpful for certain roles, we welcome volunteers with various skills and backgrounds. The most important requirements are passion, commitment, and a desire to help children.
What is the time commitment for volunteers?
Volunteer time commitments vary based on the role and your availability. We have opportunities ranging from a few hours per week to full-time involvement. We'll work with you to find a schedule that fits your life.
